What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) by the websites you visit. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make the site easier to use and more useful to you.

How we use cookies

This site uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential: Required for the site to function (for example, remembering that you accepted the cookie banner).
  • Performance & Analytics: Help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Preference: Remember choices you make (language, theme).
  • Advertising: Used only if you interact with third-party content; these cookies are controlled by those third parties.

Manage or disable cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish — for details, see aboutcookies.org. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this site.

  • To remove cookies in Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy & security > Cookies and other site data.
  • To remove cookies in Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Manage Data.
  • To remove cookies in Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data.

Third-party cookies

Some content on this site is provided by third parties (for example, analytics and social media). These services may set their own cookies. Jack Czaplewski does not control third-party cookies — please check the third party's privacy or cookie policy for more details.
